My Passions & Hobbies

Anime

I've been into anime since 2018 and absolutely been hooked every since. I love anime because it gives me an escape and actually teaches you life lessons about friendship, family, and love! It also teaches you about core values and has its funny moments. Keep scrolling to see my favorite anime!

If you want to know the history of anime, click here!

Demon Slayer

I love Demon Slayer mainly due to the main character named Tanjiro, he is unapologetically himself and has enrolled into the Demon Slayer corps to protect humans by slaying the demons that try to go after humans. I love that despite the demons intent to devour humans, he still shows empathy and prays prior to slaying them. Usually before it's time to defeat a demon, it shows why the demon ended up becoming who or what it became. The pureness of Tanjiro's heart is easily one of the many reasons why I love this anime, as well as the beautiful artwork.

Naruto

Naruto was one of the first animes that I've watched when first starting anime. This anime will always remain in my top favorite animes of all time. It's up there with the OG animes like One Piece, DBZ (Dragon Ball Z), and Bleach therefore it's a very long anime. There are two different time periods that Naruto takes place in, which is the Naruto where he was a kid growing up (which is 9 seasons long) and Naruto Shippuden, which is the one where he was a teenager growing into an adult. When you watch it from the beginning it's like you're watching Naruto grow up, along with those that are around him like friends and those who he considers family. Over time, just like with most anime of this length, you begin to feel connected to the characters and the story. This anime has gotten me through some tough times so it was always be nostalgic to me. Fun fact is, I haven't finished Shippuden yet because I'm not ready for the anime to end. However, there is Boruto which takes place after Shippuden but I've heard bad views about it due to the different writing style.

Baki

I started watching Baki this year, what inspired me to watch Baki was this movie coming out on Netflix called "Baki Hanma vs Kengan Ashura", which I was already biased about because I watched Kengan Ashura already sometime last year, but I wanted to have an unbiased view and wanted to know the strength of the other characters in Baki to make a more accurate assumption on who might win a fight against one another, you know? So I started watching Baki n and now I love it more than Kengan Ashura! I love Baki because his character is well rounded and emotionally intelligent with a great sense of humor and he cares and protects those he loves unapologetically, especially his girlfriend Yozue. Most fighting animes focus solely on fighing but in Baki there's more substance in regards to family and the connection each character has to one another, not to say Kengan Ashura isn't like that but it's more fighting focused. I haven't finished Baki yet but I'm at the end of season 2 and about to start season 3.

Video Games

I've been playing video games ever since I was a child, from the pink Nintendo DS to the Playstation 1 through Playstation 5, I've even played Xbox for a short period of time, but I will always be a Playstation girl. Below are a list of some of my favorite video games!

Persona

I love Persona because it reminds me of real life. You get to go to school, study for your classes, maintain friendships, and correct the hearts of those who are doing wrong in society and make them confess to their crimes to help the victims lives they're affecting. So it's like you're a student by day but a secret superhero at night. This is my go to game if I want to just relax and clear my mind.

Rise of the Ronin

I love Rise of Ronin because I'm really into Japanese culture and you really get immersed into it through the gameplay. You learn about Japanese history between Japan and the US, you get to create your own character (male or female) so it feels more personalized and you get to choose how history unfolds by making choices you feel is best. Each choice will have an impact on history so it makes me feel like I'm truly making a difference. I also get to look back on the choices I've made and how it inspired certain events throughout history.

Horizon Zero Dawn

I love this game because it consists of a strong female lead and how she's on a self journey to find out who she is and where she comes from. The journey shows how courages and head strong she is, and usually in games like this there's an obvious option to explore love interests between characters but not in this game which really makes it even better because it leaves more focus on the pursuit of her finding out who she really is. It's definitely a game that will pull on your heart strings.
